CareMade specializes in high-quality medical equipment and home healthcare products, including:

## 🔧 SEO Stack Overview

This repository outlines the SEO and technical foundation used to support CareMade’s eCommerce growth:

### ✅ Technical SEO
- Clean Shopify URL structure
- Canonical & index control
- Sitemap & robots.txt optimization
- Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS, INP)
- Mobile-first performance optimization

### ✅ On-Page SEO
- Keyword-focused product architecture
- Optimized meta titles & descriptions
- Structured heading hierarchy (H1–H3)
- Image optimization & ALT attributes
- Internal linking via collections & tags

### ✅ Structured Data (Schema)
- Product schema
- Aggregate rating & review schema
- Breadcrumb schema
- Organization & LocalBusiness schema
- FAQ schema for rich results

### ✅ eCommerce & Merchant Readiness
- Google Merchant Center compliance
- Product feed optimization
- Attribute mapping (GTIN, SKU, brand)
- Pricing & availability accuracy
- Shopping & free listings support

### ✅ Accessibility & Trust
- Accessibility-friendly content structure
- Clear product information
- Trust & compliance signals for medical products
- Brand entity consistency across platforms
