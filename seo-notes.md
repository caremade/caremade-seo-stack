# CareMade SEO Notes

## Website
https://caremade.ca

## SEO Objectives
- Increase organic visibility for medical equipment keywords in Canada
- Improve product discoverability
- Maintain trust and compliance for healthcare-related products

## Target Keywords
- medical equipment Canada
- mobility aids Canada
- power wheelchair Canada
- lift chair Canada
- rollator walker Canada
- mobility scooter Canada

## On-Page SEO
- One H1 per page
- Keyword-focused product titles
- Optimized meta titles and descriptions
- Image alt text for all product images
- Internal linking using collections and tags

## Technical SEO
- Clean Shopify URL structure
- Canonical tags applied correctly
- Sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
- Mobile-first optimization
- Core Web Vitals monitoring (LCP, CLS, INP)

## Structured Data
- Product schema
- Organization schema
- Review & aggregate rating schema
- Breadcrumb schema

## Compliance & Trust
- Clear product specifications
- Accurate pricing and availability
- Accessibility-friendly content
- Transparent business information
